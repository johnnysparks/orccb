   - Citations: [psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors@P9-L327-L344], [psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors@P9-L321-L323]

   - Citations: [ors-701-068@L3753-L3758], [ors-701-068@L3761-L3765]

   - Citations: [ors-701-068@L3797-L3803], [ors-701-068@L3806-L3811]

5. ORS requires liability insurance (including products/completed operations) in at least endorsement-specific amounts; evidence is required at licensure and renewal.  
   - Citations: [ors-701-073@L3874-L3882], [ors-701-073@L3885-L3888]

6. Residential endorsement examples from ORS: RGC ($25,000 bond / $500,000 insurance), RSC ($20,000 / $300,000), RLC ($15,000 / $100,000).  
   - Citations: [ors-701-081@L3932-L3976]

7. Commercial endorsement examples from ORS: CGC-1 ($80,000 bond / $2M insurance), CSC-1 ($55,000 / $1M), CGC-2 ($25,000 / $1M), CSC-2 ($25,000 / $500k).  
   - Citations: [ors-701-084@L4206-L4284]

8. OAR 812-003-0221 publishes operational insurance amounts for new/renewal applications and aligns to ORS 701.073/701.081/701.084.  
   - Citations: [oar-812-003-0221@L1657-L1667]

9. OAR 812-003-0200 requires insurance certification and specific certificate fields (insurer name, policy number, dates, required amounts, products/completed ops statement, CCB as certificate holder).  
   - Citations: [oar-812-003-0200@L1454-L1477]

10. OAR 812-003-0142 sets fee schedule updates: $400 renew/reissue effective 2024-07-01; $400 new-license applications effective 2025-07-01 (with prior $325 transition language).  
   - Citations: [oar-812-003-0142@L531-L537]

11. OAR 812-003-0260 requires detailed entity/ownership and responsible-managing-individual application disclosures, which are part of financial-administrative compliance management.  
   - Citations: [oar-812-003-0260@L2141-L2165]

12. PSI states the NASCLA Oregon guide is used to prepare exam questions, and the exam is open-book with that reference permitted.  
   - Citations: [psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors@P10-L351-L357], [psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors@P10-L353-L363]

13. Official CCB guidance reinforces that applicants must submit insurance certificates and maintain endorsement-specific coverage and bonds as part of licensing readiness.

## 3) Exact facts safe to teach (M12-boundary aware)

1. **Financial management is explicitly tested** on the Oregon CCB exam (4 scored questions).  
2. A contractor’s financial compliance obligations include maintaining required **bond and liability insurance** levels associated with endorsement type.  
3. Bond and insurance are not one-time: ORS/OAR require maintenance at licensure and renewal, and lapses/depletion can trigger enforcement consequences.  
4. Licensing fees are a budgeted operating cost, and current OAR fee tables include phased effective dates that matter for application timing.  
5. Exam prep should include open-book navigation in the NASCLA Oregon guide because PSI identifies it as the content basis and permitted reference.
