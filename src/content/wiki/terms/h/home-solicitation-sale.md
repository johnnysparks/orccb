A **home solicitation sale** is a transaction category in ORS chapter 83 with required written agreement and cancellation-notice mechanics. Source: ors-83-720, ors-83-730.

For qualifying transactions, ORS 83.720 generally allows cancellation until midnight of the third business day, with statutory exceptions. Source: ors-83-720.

In CCB training context, this framework is contrasted with the next-business-day cancellation rule in ORS 701.310 for initial written residential contracts. Source: ccb-contractor-tools, ors-701-310.
