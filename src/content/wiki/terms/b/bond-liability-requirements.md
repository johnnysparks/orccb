## Definition

**Bond and liability requirements** are the baseline financial-protection conditions tied to Oregon contractor licensing, including required bond and liability coverage alignment. Source: oar-812-003-0120.

## Why it matters

Coverage or bonding gaps can move a license out of good standing and create immediate business interruption risk.

## Where it appears in a real project

It appears at license application, renewal, project mobilization, and whenever coverage limits or carriers change.

## Inputs needed

- Current endorsement/classification context.
- Bond instrument details and effective dates.
- Liability policy information and certificates.
- Renewal and cancellation notice tracking.

## Outputs produced

- Active license compliance on bond/insurance prerequisites.
- Client-ready proof documents.
- Renewal and replacement timeline controls.

## Key decisions

- Policy structure and limit strategy.
- Renewal timing buffer before expiration.
- Internal owner for carrier and surety coordination.

## Basic workflow

1. Determine required bond/coverage profile for your license context.
2. Bind bond and liability coverage.
3. File and verify records with CCB workflow.
4. Track expirations and replacement lead times.
5. Re-verify status after any policy event.

## Risks and edge cases

- Assumptions that policy auto-renew equals uninterrupted compliance.
- Certificate mismatch with license entity details.
- Mid-project cancellation notices not escalated quickly.

## Oregon/Ashland relevance

Ashland clients and trade partners frequently request current proof documents during contract award and preconstruction. Fast retrieval supports smoother starts.

## Example scenario

A residential GC's liability policy is rewritten mid-year after adding staff. The office validates that certificates and license-facing records stay consistent before continuing new contract signings.

## Checklist

- [ ] Bond and liability documents current.
- [ ] Entity names match licensing records.
- [ ] Expiration alerts set with time buffer.
- [ ] Proof documents available for bids/contracts.
- [ ] Post-change status verification completed.
