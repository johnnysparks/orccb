**Bidding** is a scored Oregon CCB exam concept within the [[bidding-and-estimating]] domain and is treated by CCB education materials as part of construction business practices. Source: psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors, oregon-ccb-education-providers.

In practice, bidding is the step where a contractor proposes scope and pricing method before contract execution, which is why exam scenarios often connect bidding choices to later compliance duties. Source: psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors, oregon-ccb-education-providers.

See [[estimating]] and [[fixed-price-contract]].
