**Bond Penal Sum** is the maximum amount available from a contractor license bond for covered obligations. Source: ors-701-068.

Under ORS 701.068, if paid bond obligations exceed the penal sum, CCB may suspend the license and require a larger bond for reinstatement, potentially up to five times the normal bond amount. Source: ors-701-068.

See [[bond-requirements]] and [[financial-management]].
