A **Business Identification Number (BIN)** is Oregon's employer tax account number used to manage payroll tax reporting and withholding programs. Source: oregon-dor-withholding-and-payroll-tax.

Oregon DOR states employers with paid employees working in Oregon should register for a BIN before issuing paychecks. Source: oregon-dor-withholding-and-payroll-tax.

CCB licensing guidance also flags tax-account setup as part of the licensing preparation workflow. Source: oregon-ccb-license-page.
