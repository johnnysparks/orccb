A **breach of contract** occurs when a party does not perform a contractual duty without legal excuse. Source: psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors.

Exam-prep materials distinguish major breaches from minor breaches because available remedies can differ by severity. Source: nascla-contractors-guide-or-2e.
