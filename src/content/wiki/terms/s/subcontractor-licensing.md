**Subcontractor Licensing** means a contractor generally may not hire another contractor unless the hired party is licensed and properly endorsed for the work, or exempt under ORS 701.010. Source: ors-701-026, ors-701-010.

This rule sits on top of Oregon's broader licensing baseline in ORS 701.021, so compliance checks should happen before subcontracted work starts. Source: ors-701-021, ors-701-026.
