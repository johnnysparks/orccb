**Statewide Transit Tax (STT)** is Oregon's statewide employee-wage withholding tax framework, referenced by DOR as enacted under ORS 320.550. Source: oregon-dor-statewide-transit-tax, ors-320-550.

DOR explains that STT withholding applies to Oregon residents (regardless of work location) and to nonresidents for services performed in Oregon, with employer responsibility for withholding, reporting, and remittance. Source: oregon-dor-statewide-transit-tax.

DOR also ties STT wage scope to ORS 316.162 and distinguishes self-employment income from STT withholding treatment. Source: oregon-dor-statewide-transit-tax, ors-316-162.
