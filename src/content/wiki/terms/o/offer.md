An **offer** is a definite proposal to contract that can be accepted as presented. Source: psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors.

In exam scenarios, changing terms usually becomes a counterproposal instead of acceptance of the original offer. Source: nascla-contractors-guide-or-2e.

See [[acceptance]] and [[consideration]].
