The **owner responsibility notice** is the CCB-adopted information notice and statement used for owner-side acknowledgments when an exemption pathway is used at residential permit issuance. Source: ors-701-325, oar-812-001-0200.

The CCB form states owner commitments, including that hired contractors and subcontractors must be licensed and properly endorsed when the owner hires out work. Source: ccb-owner-construction-responsibilities-notice.

The same CCB notice form states it is required for residential building, electrical, mechanical, and plumbing permit workflows. Verify current local implementation steps with the issuing jurisdiction because agency process details can vary. Source: ccb-owner-construction-responsibilities-notice.
