A **change order** documents changes in scope, price, or schedule after the original agreement is made. Source: ccb-contractor-tools.

For projects governed by Oregon written-contract compliance rules, documenting pricing and scope changes in writing supports consistent compliance and dispute prevention. Source: ors-701-305, ccb-contractor-tools.
