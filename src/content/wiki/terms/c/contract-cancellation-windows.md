In Oregon residential contracting, cancellation timing depends on which statute governs the transaction, not just on the fact that the project is a home remodel. Source: ors-701-310, ors-83-720, ccb-contractor-tools.

For an initial written residential construction contract under ORS 701.310, an owner generally may cancel until midnight of the next business day, subject to exceptions in the statute. Source: ors-701-310.

For a qualifying [[home-solicitation-sale]] under ORS 83.720 and ORS 83.730, cancellation is generally available through midnight of the third business day, with specific notice mechanics and statutory exceptions. Source: ors-83-720, ors-83-730.

Exam prep tip: classify the fact pattern first, then apply the matching cancellation window to avoid mixing the one-day and three-day frameworks. Source: ccb-contractor-tools, ors-701-310, ors-83-720.
