**Corporate Activity Tax (CAT)** is an Oregon business tax structure tied to taxable Oregon commercial activity under ORS chapter 317A and related DOR guidance. Source: oregon-dor-corporate-activity-tax, ors-chapter-317a.

Oregon DOR states CAT registration generally begins at $750,000 in Oregon commercial activity, while filing/payment duties apply above $1 million of taxable Oregon commercial activity. Source: oregon-dor-corporate-activity-tax.

DOR describes CAT tax computation at $250 plus 0.57% of taxable Oregon commercial activity above the $1 million threshold, and notes return timing as the 15th day of the fourth month after tax-year end. Source: oregon-dor-corporate-activity-tax.
