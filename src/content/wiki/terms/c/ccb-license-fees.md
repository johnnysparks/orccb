**CCB license fees** are the amounts a contractor must pay when applying for a new license, renewing an existing license, or reissuing a license. OAR 812-003-0142 establishes the fee schedule and governs its effective dates. Source: oar-812-003-0142.

### Current fee schedule

Under OAR 812-003-0142 as updated:

| Transaction | Fee | Effective Date |
|---|---|---|
| Renewal or reissue | $400 | 2024-07-01 |
| New license application | $400 | 2025-07-01 |
| New license application (prior) | $325 | Before 2025-07-01 |

Source: oar-812-003-0142.

### Exam context

Fee effective dates are exam-testable because the phased implementation means the correct fee depends on when the transaction occurs. An application filed before July 1, 2025 used the $325 amount; one filed after that date uses $400. Understanding this distinction prevents an application-timing error. Source: psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors.

Fees are also a practical financial planning item: they are a recurring operating cost that must be budgeted as part of licensing continuity. Source: oar-812-003-0142.
