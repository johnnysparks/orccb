**Consideration** is the legal value each party gives or promises in exchange for the other party's promise. Source: psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors.

Typical construction consideration is work performance on one side and payment on the other. Source: nascla-contractors-guide-or-2e.

See [[offer]] and [[acceptance]].
