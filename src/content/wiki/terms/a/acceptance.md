**Acceptance** is agreement to an offer on the same terms presented by the offeror. Source: psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors.

If a party changes terms while responding, the response is treated as a counteroffer in standard contract framing used for exam prep. Source: nascla-contractors-guide-or-2e.

See [[offer]] and [[consideration]].
