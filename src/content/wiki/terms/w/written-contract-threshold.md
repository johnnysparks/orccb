Oregon's residential **written contract threshold** is crossed when aggregate contract price exceeds $2,000 under ORS 701.305. Source: ors-701-305.

If price starts at or below $2,000 and later exceeds that amount, ORS 701.305 requires mailing or delivery of the written contract within five days after the contractor knew or should have known the threshold was exceeded. Source: ors-701-305.

CCB guidance pairs this threshold with required consumer notices and two-year proof-retention expectations for qualifying jobs. Source: ccb-contractor-tools, ccb-required-notices-chart.
