**Exterior Wall Envelope** refers to wall-system requirements intended to resist weather exposure and manage moisture at the building exterior, including code concepts tied to ORSC R703 references. Source: oregon-bcd-2021-orsc-significant-changes.

Because Oregon code editions are updated in adoption cycles, contractors should confirm current adoption status before applying detailed section language from prior cycles. Source: oregon-bcd-orsc-adoption.
