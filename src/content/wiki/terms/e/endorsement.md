In Oregon CCB licensing, an **endorsement** is the license authorization that must match the kind of contractor work being offered, bid, or performed. Source: ors-701-021.

When hiring subcontractors, endorsement fit matters as much as license status because ORS 701.026 requires the hired party to be properly licensed and endorsed for the work unless exempt. Source: ors-701-026.

See [[ccb-license]], [[subcontractor-licensing]], and [[working-with-subcontractors]].
