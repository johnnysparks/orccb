## Definition

**Entity setup** is the implementation phase of your business structure decision: forming the entity, aligning ownership/authority records, and connecting that legal identity to contractor licensing records. Source: oregon-ccb-guide-becoming-licensed-contractor.

## Why it matters

A strong setup prevents downstream mismatches between contracts, certificates, invoices, and license data.

## Where it appears in a real project

It appears at first contract signing, permit applications, and any administrative review where official business identity must match.

## Inputs needed

- Selected business structure.
- Registered business details.
- Authorized signer and management roles.
- Licensing application identity fields.

## Outputs produced

- Consistent legal identity across operational documents.
- Reduced rework in permit and contract administration.
- Cleaner renewal/change management.

## Key decisions

- Naming conventions across all systems.
- Who can sign and bind the company.
- How updates are controlled when ownership changes.

## Basic workflow

1. Finalize structure and registration.
2. Standardize legal name/address usage.
3. Configure licensing records to match entity records.
4. Align contract templates and certificates.
5. Audit consistency quarterly.

## Risks and edge cases

- "DBA" usage that diverges from licensing record details.
- Staff using outdated templates with old entity information.
- Ownership changes without parallel record updates.

## Oregon/Ashland relevance

Ashland homeowners and local process stakeholders often compare contract and permit records closely; consistent entity presentation reduces trust and processing friction.

## Example scenario

A remodel firm converts from sole proprietor to LLC. The team updates license-facing records, contract headers, insurance certificates, and invoice templates before launching new bids.

## Checklist

- [ ] Entity records finalized.
- [ ] Name/address/signature authority standardized.
- [ ] License records aligned.
- [ ] Contract and billing templates updated.
- [ ] Quarterly consistency audit scheduled.
