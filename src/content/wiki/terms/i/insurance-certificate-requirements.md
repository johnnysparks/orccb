**Insurance Certificate Requirements** are the rule-based data elements and statements required as proof of contractor liability insurance for CCB licensing. Source: oar-812-003-0200.

OAR 812-003-0200 specifies required certificate information, including insurer identity, policy number and dates, required coverage amounts, products/completed operations language, and CCB certificate-holder details. Source: oar-812-003-0200.

OAR 812-003-0221 should be used with this rule to confirm the required amount by endorsement type. Source: oar-812-003-0221.
