**License Application Fee** is the CCB fee amount set by rule for new applications, renewals, and related licensing transactions. Source: oar-812-003-0142.

OAR 812-003-0142 includes effective-date-based fee updates, including $400 for renewal/reissue transactions effective July 1, 2024, and $400 for new-license applications effective July 1, 2025. Source: oar-812-003-0142.

See [[ccb-license]] and [[financial-management]].
