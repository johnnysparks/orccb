**Liquidated damages** are pre-agreed contract amounts used when specified nonperformance occurs, commonly delay-related in construction examples. Source: nascla-contractors-guide-or-2e.

Exam framing treats these as contract remedies tied to breach analysis. Source: psi-cib-oregon-construction-contractors.
