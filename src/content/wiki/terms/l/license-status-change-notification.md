When a contractor's operating facts change between exempt and nonexempt, Oregon rules require a **Notification of Changed Contractor License Status** filing with CCB. Source: oar-812-003-0321.

The filing deadline is within 10 days of the status change. Missing that window creates compliance risk because status misalignment can overlap with statutory discipline grounds. Source: oar-812-003-0321, ors-701-098.

Common trigger events include starting to hire employees or beginning to use worker-leasing workers while previously licensed exempt. Source: ors-701-035, oar-812-003-0250.

Use this term with [[exempt-vs-nonexempt]], [[worker-leasing]], and [[ccb-license]] when analyzing exam scenarios about staffing transitions.
