**Lien perfection** means completing statutory steps needed for an enforceable construction lien, including filing requirements under ORS 87.035 and post-filing notice under ORS 87.039. Source: ors-chapter-87.

A core exam timing pattern is the ORS 87.035 filing framework (commonly taught as 75 days) plus the separate 20-day post-filing notice duty. Source: ors-chapter-87.

Even if filing steps are timely, ORS 701.131 can still limit perfection when licensing conditions are not satisfied. Source: ors-701-131.

Continue to [[lien-foreclosure]] for enforcement deadlines.
