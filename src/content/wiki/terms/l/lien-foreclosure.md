**Lien foreclosure** is the lawsuit process used to enforce a perfected construction lien against property. Source: ors-chapter-87.

Under ORS 87.055, a construction lien generally does not bind longer than 120 days after filing unless suit is brought as required, and ORS 87.057 requires at least 10 days' notice of intent to foreclose before suit. Source: ors-chapter-87.
