A **fixed-price contract** sets a defined overall project price at contract formation, which can make total-cost expectations clearer than open-ended billing models. Source: oregon-ccb-guide-to-hiring-a-contractor.

For Oregon exam scenarios, compare this structure against [[time-and-materials]] and remember that later scope changes may still require formal [[change-order]] handling under contract law principles. Source: oregon-ccb-guide-to-hiring-a-contractor.
