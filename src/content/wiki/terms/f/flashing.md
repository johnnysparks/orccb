**Flashing** is integrated transition detailing at joints, penetrations, and material interfaces that helps direct water to the exterior in wall-envelope assemblies, consistent with Oregon ORSC R703 references. Source: oregon-bcd-2021-orsc-significant-changes.

For exam study and field application, flashing should be understood as part of a system with the weather-resistant envelope and drainage pathway concepts. Source: oregon-bcd-2021-orsc-significant-changes.
