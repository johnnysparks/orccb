**Permit license verification** is the permit-counter requirement that a building permit cannot be issued to a person who is required to hold a CCB license unless that person has a current, valid, properly endorsed license. Source: ors-701-325.

ORS 701.325 also requires a signed applicant statement at issuance: contractors identify their CCB license number, while exempt applicants identify their exemption basis. Source: ors-701-325.

This verification step is closely tied to [[building-permits]], [[ccb-license]], and [[owner-builder]], because the same permit transaction determines whether a license must be present or an exemption path must be documented. Source: ors-701-325, ors-701-010.
