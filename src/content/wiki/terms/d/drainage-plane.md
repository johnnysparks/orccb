**Drainage Plane** is the exterior-envelope concept that incidental water should have a designed path to drain outward rather than accumulate within wall assemblies, as reflected in Oregon ORSC envelope references. Source: oregon-bcd-2021-orsc-significant-changes.

This concept is commonly taught alongside weather-resistive and flashing integration rules so water management works as a complete system. Source: oregon-bcd-2021-orsc-significant-changes.
