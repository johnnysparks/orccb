In Oregon contractor exam scenarios, a **right of rescission** means a statutory cancellation right after contract signing when the governing law grants one. Source: ors-701-310, ors-83-720.

For initial written residential contracts under ORS 701.310, cancellation is generally available until midnight of the next business day, subject to listed exceptions. Source: ors-701-310.

For qualifying [[home-solicitation-sale]] transactions, ORS 83.720 and ORS 83.730 provide a generally longer three-business-day cancellation framework with required notice mechanics and exceptions. Source: ors-83-720, ors-83-730.

See [[contracts]] and [[home-solicitation-sale]].
