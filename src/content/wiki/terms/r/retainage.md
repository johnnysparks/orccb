**Retainage** is a portion of payment withheld until contract milestones, often substantial completion, are met. Source: nascla-contractors-guide-or-2e.

In Oregon contractor practice guidance, retainage is treated as a payment-control and completion-assurance tool inside written contract administration. Source: ccb-contractor-tools.
